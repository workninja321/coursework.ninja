What to include in your first message

To help you faster, share the basics up front. We’ll respond with a clear plan and next steps.

  • Program + course code (example: “WGU C779”)
  • Assessment type (OA, PA, or project)
  • Deadline (and your timezone)
  • What you’ve tried (so we don’t repeat steps)
  • Where you’re stuck (1–2 sentences is fine)
