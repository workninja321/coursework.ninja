Full Capstone Support From Start to Finish

Our comprehensive WGU capstone guide covers the process in detail. When you need hands-on support to execute your capstone, we step in and help you finish with confidence.

We help with every stage of the capstone process:

  • Topic Selection: Finding a focused, feasible topic that demonstrates program competencies.
  • Proposal Development: Writing a clear proposal that gets approved quickly.
  • Literature Review: Identifying and synthesizing relevant research sources.
  • Methodology: Designing your approach and justifying your methods.
  • Implementation: Executing your project and documenting results.
  • Final Documentation: Comprehensive write-up meeting all rubric requirements.

Common Capstone Challenges We Help Solve

Students often get stuck at specific points in the capstone process. We help overcome these common challenges:

  • Topic too broad: We help narrow your focus to something achievable.
  • Unclear rubric requirements: We decode what evaluators actually want.
  • Literature review struggles: We help find and synthesize relevant sources.
  • Methodology confusion: We guide appropriate research design.
  • Revision fatigue: We address feedback systematically to pass on resubmission.
