What the Degree Completion Service Covers

When you partner with us for degree completion, you get comprehensive support across every aspect of your remaining program:

All Remaining Courses

Whether you have 5 courses left or 25, we handle them all. Our subject-matter experts complete Performance Assessments (written assignments), prepare you for Objective Assessments (proctored exams), and manage discussion posts and other requirements. Each course is assigned to an expert in that specific field—MBA courses go to MBAs, IT courses to certified professionals, nursing courses to RNs.

Proctored Exam Support

OAs are often the biggest obstacle to degree completion. Our exam support includes:

  • Targeted preparation: Study materials focused on exactly what your specific OA tests
  • Practice tests: Realistic exams that mirror the actual assessment
  • Coaching sessions: One-on-one help understanding difficult concepts
  • Exam-day support: From confidence boosting to comprehensive assistance

Certification Exams

Many WGU programs include industry certifications like CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, AWS certifications, Project+, and others. These are often the most challenging exams in your program. Our certified professionals provide specialized preparation and support for all bundled certifications.

Your Capstone Project

The capstone is the final hurdle before graduation. Our capstone specialists handle everything:

  • Topic selection and approval
  • Proposal development (Task 1)
  • Main project completion (Task 2)
  • Reflection and documentation (Task 3)
  • Revision support if evaluators request changes
