Admissions & entry exams

  • Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution required for MBA admission.
  • No GMAT or GRE required for WGU business programs.
  • Your enrollment counselor confirms transfer credits and start timeline.

Assessments & certifications

  • Course assessments focus on applied performance work and real-world business scenarios.
  • The MBA Capstone is a culminating assessment that brings the program together.
  • No third-party certifications are listed in the official MBA program guide.

Suggested roadmap phases

Your mentor may adjust the sequence, but these phases mirror the program guide course list and learning flow.

Phase 1: Leadership foundations

Managing Organizations and Leading People, Managing Human Capital, Management Communication.

Phase 2: Business fundamentals

Marketing, Accounting for Decision Makers, Financial Management.

Phase 3: Strategy & analytics

Ethical Leadership, Operations Management, Data-Driven Decision Making, Global Economics for Managers.

Phase 4: Capstone

MBA Capstone.
