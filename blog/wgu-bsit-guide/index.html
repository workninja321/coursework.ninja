WGU BSIT: How to Complete Your IT Degree in Record Time

December 17, 2025 10 min read Degree Guides

The WGU Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) is one of the most popular and affordable paths to an IT career. What makes it exceptional? You'll earn multiple industry certifications included in your tuition while completing a fully accredited degree at your own pace.

This guide covers everything you need to know: program structure, included certifications, costs, and proven strategies to accelerate through your IT degree.

WGU BSIT Program Overview

The WGU BSIT is a 36-course program designed with input from IT industry experts. It covers five primary areas: systems and services, networking and security, scripting and programming, data management, and the business of IT.

Unlike traditional universities, WGU uses a competency-based model. You advance by demonstrating mastery through assessments, not by sitting through lectures for a set number of weeks. This means experienced IT professionals can accelerate through familiar material while spending more time on new concepts.

How to Accelerate Your BSIT

Completing your BSIT in one or two terms is achievable with the right strategy. Here's how successful accelerators approach the program:

Before Enrollment

  1. Maximize transfer credits: Complete general education courses through Sophia Learning ($99/month unlimited) or Study.com before enrolling
  2. Earn transferable certifications: CompTIA A+ and other certs can transfer as course credit
  3. Assess your knowledge: If you have IT work experience, you may already know material covered in many courses

During Your Program

  • Take pre-assessments immediately: Many courses can be passed quickly if you already know the material
  • Use community resources: Reddit's r/WGU, Discord servers, and Quizlet have course-specific tips
  • Communicate with your mentor: Request to add courses once you demonstrate progress
  • Study strategically: Focus on certification exam objectives for cert-based courses
Certification Reality Check: The certification exams (especially Security+) are the most challenging part of the program. Budget extra time for these courses. Many students find the general education and written papers easier than the technical certifications.

Admission Requirements

WGU BSIT admission is straightforward with multiple pathways:

  • Bachelor's or Associate degree from an accredited institution
  • OR Transferable IT certification (like CompTIA A+)
  • OR High school transcript with 3.0+ GPA
  • OR Previous IT coursework at 300+ level

No SAT, ACT, or GRE required. You'll complete a brief admissions interview to discuss your goals and readiness.

Career Outcomes

The BSIT prepares you for various IT roles, and the included certifications make you immediately marketable:

  • Help Desk/Technical Support: Entry point with A+ certification
  • Network Administrator: Network+ and Security+ qualifications
  • Systems Administrator: Linux and cloud foundations
  • Cloud Engineer: AWS Cloud Practitioner as starting point
  • IT Project Manager: Project+ and ITIL foundations
  • Security Analyst: Security+ certification pathway

WGU has partnerships with major employers including Amazon, Google, Microsoft, and Disney who recognize WGU credentials.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most students complete the WGU BSIT in 2-3 years, though accelerators with IT experience and transfer credits can finish in as little as one 6-month term. According to WGU, 61% of BSIT graduates finish within 39 months.
