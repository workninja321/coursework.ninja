WGU MBA Program: Complete Guide to Fast-Track Your Business Degree

December 17, 2025 12 min read Degree Guides

The Western Governors University MBA is one of the most affordable and flexible graduate business degrees available. With WGU's competency-based model, motivated students can complete their MBA in as little as 6-12 months, saving thousands compared to traditional programs.

This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about the WGU MBA: program structure, courses, costs, acceleration strategies, and tips from successful graduates.

WGU MBA Program Overview

The WGU Master of Business Administration is a 36 competency unit (CU) program designed for working professionals. Unlike traditional MBA programs with fixed schedules, WGU allows you to move through courses at your own pace.

Key Program Features

  • Competency-Based: Advance by demonstrating mastery, not seat time
  • Flat-Rate Tuition: Take as many courses as you can per 6-month term
  • No GMAT Required: Admission based on bachelor's degree and interview
  • ACBSP Accredited: Recognized business program accreditation
  • 100% Online: Study anytime, anywhere
  • Mentor Support: Dedicated program mentor throughout your journey

How to Accelerate Through the WGU MBA

Completing the WGU MBA in one term (6 months) is absolutely achievable with the right strategy. Here's how successful accelerators approach the program:

Before You Start

  • Check for transfer credits: Prior graduate courses may transfer
  • Leverage your experience: Business professionals often move faster
  • Clear your schedule: Plan for 20-30 hours/week of study time
  • Set a term goal: Decide how many courses you want to complete
Important: WGU restricts you to one active course at a time initially. Once you demonstrate competency, your mentor can approve adding additional courses. Build trust early by completing courses efficiently.

WGU MBA Career Outcomes

A WGU MBA can significantly impact your career. Here's what graduates report:

Admission Requirements

Basic Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree: From a regionally accredited institution
  • Minimum GPA: No minimum GPA requirement
  • GMAT/GRE: Not required
  • Work experience: Not required (but helpful for acceleration)

Application Process

  1. Submit online application
  2. Provide official transcripts
  3. Complete admissions interview
  4. Receive acceptance decision
  5. Choose your start date (1st of each month)

Frequently Asked Questions

The WGU MBA can be completed in as little as 6 months if you can dedicate significant time to studying. On average, most students complete the program in 12-18 months. WGU's competency-based model allows you to accelerate through courses where you already have knowledge and experience.

The WGU MBA costs approximately $4,530 per 6-month term (as of 2025). Since you can take as many courses as you can complete each term, the total cost ranges from $4,530 (one term) to around $18,000+ depending on your pace. Most students complete it in 2-3 terms ($9,000-$13,500).

Yes, the WGU MBA is accredited by ACBSP (Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs) and WGU is regionally accredited by NWCCU. Major employers including Amazon, Google, and Microsoft have partnerships with WGU.

WGU MBA admission requires a bachelor's degree from an accredited institution. There is no GMAT or GRE requirement. You'll also need to complete a brief admissions interview. Professional experience is not required but is beneficial for accelerating through the program.
